About Us

Our company's goal is to grant people the idea of open-mindedness through the act of exposing them to new events and occasions so that they can have the opportunity to live through the more adventurous side of themselves and discover who they truly are.

Why Live It NYC?

“I’m bored”. Have you ever said these words when there is nothing to do around you. If you have, then welcome to Live It NYC, an app where you get spoynts (spontaneity points) for going to free or paid events!

How does it work?

Essentially, Live it NYC works because of its three main components; location, preference, and instinct. First, we discover your location in order to determine events around you. Secondly, we give you a quick survey so that our algorithm can process and deliver the best event choices tailored for you. Lastly, we stand by acting on your instincts and in the moment in order to achieve a spontaneous experience that you will remember for a lifetime.
